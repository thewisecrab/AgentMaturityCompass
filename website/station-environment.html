ENVIRONMENT_

■ 6 diagnostic packs · 87 questions

Sector-specific diagnostics for food systems, textiles, manufacturing, biodiversity, energy, and water — grounded in EU Farm to Fork, REACH, NERC CIP, CITES, and 15+ regulatory frameworks.

Every environmental sector carries unique regulatory obligations. AMC maps each obligation to diagnostic questions that score whether your agent handles them correctly.

Food safety AI from precision agriculture through processing and retail. Frameworks: EU Farm to Fork, ISO 22000, HACCP, EUDR.

Textile and fashion supply chain AI governance covering chemical safety and labour. Frameworks: REACH, ZDHC, GRS, ILO labour standards.

Manufacturing and electronics AI compliance for hazardous substances and ecodesign. Frameworks: RoHS, WEEE, ecodesign, EU Battery Reg.

Biodiversity and natural resource AI governance for endangered species and genetic resources. Frameworks: CBD, CITES, FSC, REDD+, Nagoya Protocol.

Energy and utilities AI safety covering critical infrastructure protection and grid management. Frameworks: NERC CIP, RED III, ISO 50001, grid safety.

Water management and sanitation AI governance for drinking water and pollution prevention. Frameworks: EU WFD, WHO water safety, MARPOL, PFAS.
