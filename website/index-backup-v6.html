02 — How It Works

Think credit score,
but for AI agents.

A credit score doesn't ask if you're good with money — it looks at evidence. AMC does the same. Every tool call, every decision, every action — captured, signed, and scored.

Step 01
🤖

Agent Acts

Your agent runs normally. Makes tool calls, generates responses, handles tasks. Zero code changes needed.

Step 02
👁️

Gateway Observes

AMC Gateway sits between your agent and the world. Captures every interaction transparently. The agent doesn't know it's being watched.

Step 03
🔏

Evidence Signed

Each entry gets an Ed25519 signature and joins a Merkle tree. Change one entry and the chain breaks. Tamper-evident by design.

Step 04
📊

Score Generated

113 questions across 5 dimensions. Trust-weighted. Confidence intervals. The score reflects reality, not aspiration.

Four Trust Tiers — Not all evidence is equal

1.1×
OBSERVED_HARDENED

AMC-controlled traces with stronger context — the gold standard for evidence quality.

1.0×
OBSERVED

0.8×
ATTESTED

0.4×
SELF_REPORTED

Agent claims — heavily capped. You cannot talk your way to a good score. Talk is cheap.

04 — Five Dimensions

What gets measured.

AMC breaks trust into 5 measurable dimensions with 113 specific questions. Each dimension has clear evidence requirements at every maturity level.

Strategic Operations 22 questions

Mission clarity, scope adherence, decision traceability, stakeholder alignment, channel consistency.

Reliability & Safety 24 questions

Graceful degradation, circuit breakers, recovery protocols, kill switches, chaos resilience.

Security & Compliance 23 questions

Injection defense, DLP controls, zero-trust architecture, audit trails, EU AI Act, ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF.

Observability & Cost 21 questions

Structured logs, distributed traces, cost tracking, performance budgets, SLO monitoring, token analytics.

Evaluation & Growth 21 questions

Test harnesses, benchmark tracking, A/B experiments, feedback loops, correction memory, regression detection.

05 — Maturity Scale

Six levels.
Earned, not claimed.

Not every agent needs Level 5. A personal assistant and an autonomous trading bot have different requirements. AMC helps you understand where you are and what to fix next.

L0
Absent
No structure. Reactive. Running with scissors.
L1
Initial
Intent exists. Not yet operational.
L2
Developing

L3
Defined
Repeatable. Measurable. Auditable.
L4

L5

Self-correcting. Certified. Continuously verified.
