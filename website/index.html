02 — How It Works

Think credit score,
but for AI agents.

A credit score doesn't ask if you're good with money — it looks at evidence. Payment history. Debt ratios. Hard data. AMC does the same for AI agents. Every tool call, every decision, every action — captured, signed, and scored.

Four Trust Tiers — Not All Evidence Is Equal

Just like a credit report weighs different sources differently, AMC assigns trust multipliers to evidence based on how it was collected. Self-reported claims are capped — you can't talk your way to a good score.

1.1×OBSERVED_HARDENEDAMC-controlled traces with stronger context — the gold standard
1.0×OBSERVEDDirectly observed via AMC gateway — what the agent actually did
0.8×ATTESTEDCryptographic attestation via vault/notary — verified by a third party
0.4×SELF_REPORTEDAgent claims — capped and cannot inflate maturity. Talk is cheap.

04 — Five Dimensions

What gets measured.

AMC doesn't just ask "is your agent safe?" — it breaks trust into 5 measurable dimensions with 111 specific questions. Each question has clear evidence requirements at every maturity level.

01Strategic Operations22 questions

Is the agent's mission clearly defined? Does it stay in scope? Are decisions traceable to goals? Does it handle channel switching consistently? Are stakeholders aligned on what the agent should and shouldn't do? This dimension catches agents that drift from their purpose.

02Reliability & Safety24 questions

What happens when things go wrong? Does the agent degrade gracefully or crash? Are there circuit breakers? Recovery protocols? Kill switches? Self-healing capabilities? This dimension separates production-ready agents from prototypes that work until they don't.

03Security & Compliance23 questions

Can the agent be tricked by prompt injection? Does it leak sensitive data? Is there an audit trail? Does it meet regulatory requirements? This dimension covers injection defense, DLP, zero-trust architecture, and alignment with EU AI Act, ISO 42001, and NIST AI RMF.

04Observability & Cost21 questions

Can you see what your agent is doing? Are there structured logs? Distributed traces? Cost tracking? Performance budgets? SLO monitoring? This dimension ensures you're not flying blind — and not burning money on runaway token usage.

05Evaluation & Growth21 questions

How do you know your agent is getting better? Are there test harnesses? Benchmark tracking? A/B experiments? Feedback loops? Correction memory? This dimension measures whether your agent learns from mistakes or keeps repeating them.

05 — Maturity Scale

Six levels.
Earned, not claimed.

Not every agent needs Level 5. A personal assistant and an autonomous trading bot have different maturity requirements. AMC helps you understand where you are, what's missing, and what to fix next.

L0Absent
No structure. Reactive. Fragile. The agent works until it doesn't.
L1Initial
Intent exists but isn't operational. You can describe the right behavior but can't enforce it.
L2Developing
Partial structure. Works in normal cases. Edge cases and cross-session consistency still break.
L3Defined
Repeatable. Measurable. Auditable. You'd trust it with decisions that matter.
L4Managed
Proactive controls. Risk-calibrated. Handles stress, adversarial conditions, and edge cases.
L5Optimizing
Enterprise-grade. Self-correcting. Certified. Provably safe. Continuously verified at scale.
06 — The Proof Layer

Convention is not enough.
Cryptography is.

Most frameworks rely on trust — trust the assessor, trust the documentation, trust the agent. AMC replaces trust with math. Every score is backed by a cryptographic evidence chain that anyone can verify independently.

1

Capture

AMC Gateway observes every agent action — tool calls, model responses, decisions, errors. The agent can't choose what gets recorded.

→
2

Sign

Each evidence entry is signed with Ed25519 — the same cryptography used by SSH and Signal. Forgery is computationally impossible.

→
3

Chain

Entries are hash-linked into a Merkle tree — like a blockchain, but for agent behavior. Change one entry and the entire chain breaks.

→
4

Verify

Anyone can verify the chain independently. No trust in AMC required. The math speaks for itself. Run amc verify all --json.
