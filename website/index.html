02 — How It Works

Think credit score,
but for AI agents.

A credit score doesn't ask if you're good with money — it looks at evidence. AMC does the same. Every tool call, every decision, every action — captured, signed, and scored.

How does AMC
actually work?

Just like your credit score tracks your financial behavior without asking you, AMC watches what an AI actually does. It records every action, checks if the AI followed the rules, and gives it a score from 0 to 5. Simple as that.

Agent Acts

Your agent runs normally. Makes tool calls, generates responses, handles tasks. No code changes needed.

AI Does Its Thing

Your AI works exactly like normal. Nothing changes for it.

Gateway Observes

AMC Gateway sits between your agent and the world. Captures every interaction transparently. The agent doesn't know it's being watched.

AMC Watches

AMC sits in the middle and records everything the AI does — like a security camera it can't see.

Evidence Signed

Each entry gets an Ed25519 signature and joins a Merkle tree. Like a blockchain for behavior — change one entry and the chain breaks.

Proof Locked

Every action gets a tamper-proof stamp. Nobody can change the record — not even us.

Score Generated

111 questions across 5 dimensions. Trust-weighted. Confidence intervals. The score reflects reality, not aspiration.

Score Given

Your AI gets a trust score from 0 to 5 based on what it actually did. Honest and automatic.

Four Trust Tiers — Not All Evidence Is Equal

Just like a credit report weighs sources differently, AMC assigns trust multipliers based on how evidence was collected. Self-reported claims are capped — you can't talk your way to a good score.

1.1× OBSERVED_HARDENED
AMC-controlled traces with stronger context — the gold standard
1.0× OBSERVED
Directly observed via AMC gateway — what the agent actually did
0.8× ATTESTED
Cryptographic attestation via vault/notary — verified by a third party
0.4× SELF_REPORTED
Agent claims — capped and cannot inflate maturity. Talk is cheap.

08 — The Proof Layer

Convention is not enough.
Cryptography is.

Most frameworks rely on trust. AMC replaces trust with math. Every score is backed by a cryptographic evidence chain that anyone can verify independently.

Every score comes
with proof.

When AMC gives your AI a score, it's not just a number — it comes with a tamper-proof receipt. Think of it like a blockchain for AI behavior. Nobody can fake it, and anyone can check it.

Evidence Entry

Tool call, model response, decision, error — each becomes a signed record

sha256: 9c4e…

Merkle Tree

Entries hash-link into a tree. Change one entry and every parent hash breaks.

root: a7f0…

Ed25519 Signature

Same cryptography as SSH and Signal. Forgery is computationally impossible.

sig: Ed25519

Verified Score

Anyone can verify independently. No trust in AMC required. The math speaks.

amc verify all ✓
