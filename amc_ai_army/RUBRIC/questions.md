# AMC Compass Sprint — First-Pass Maturity Assessment Questions

Use this in discovery calls/workshops to score current-state maturity across core operating domains.

## How to use
- Score each question **1–5** using `RUBRIC/scoring_guide.md`
- Capture evidence in real time (links, screenshots, docs, KPI exports)
- Mark confidence: **High** (verified), **Medium** (partially verified), **Low** (self-reported only)
- If a question is not applicable, mark **N/A** and note why

---

## Domain A: Strategy & Business Alignment
1. How clearly are AI/automation priorities tied to top business outcomes (revenue, margin, cycle time, retention)?
2. Is there a documented 90-day operating plan with owners, milestones, and expected KPI impact?
3. How consistently does leadership review AI/ops initiatives and make decisions based on performance data?
4. Are investment decisions (tools, staffing, vendors) made through a shared ROI framework?

## Domain B: Process Standardization & SOP Quality
5. Are core service/GTM/ops workflows documented end-to-end (including handoffs)?
6. How standardized are workflows across teams/locations/accounts (vs. each person doing it differently)?
7. Are SOPs version-controlled, easy to find, and actively maintained?
8. Are exception paths (edge cases, escalations, failure handling) clearly defined?

## Domain C: Data Quality & Instrumentation
9. How reliable is source-of-truth data for key workflows (CRM, PM, support, finance, ops systems)?
10. Are critical fields, tags, and event data complete and consistently used?
11. How quickly can the team produce baseline metrics for priority workflows without manual cleanup?
12. Are dashboards trusted and used in weekly decision-making?

## Domain D: Tooling & Systems Integration
13. How well integrated are core systems (CRM, marketing, service delivery, support, finance, internal knowledge)?
14. Are automations reliable and observable (alerts, logs, ownership, SLA for fixes)?
15. How much work depends on brittle manual copy/paste between systems?
16. Are AI tools selected and configured for repeatable production use (not ad-hoc experimentation only)?

## Domain E: Governance, Risk & Security
17. Are there clear policies for data handling, model/tool usage, and prompt/automation guardrails?
18. Is access control managed via roles/least privilege and reviewed regularly?
19. Are there review/approval checkpoints for high-risk outputs (client-facing, legal/compliance, financial)?
20. Are incidents, errors, and policy violations tracked with corrective action loops?

## Domain F: Team Adoption, Enablement & Change Readiness
21. Do teams have role-specific training for new SOPs, automations, and AI-assisted workflows?
22. How consistently are new behaviors adopted in day-to-day execution?
23. Is there a clear ownership model (executive sponsor + operator owner + frontline champions)?
24. Are resistance points surfaced and addressed quickly (feedback loops, office hours, coaching)?

## Domain G: Performance Management & Continuous Improvement
25. Are workflow KPIs defined with clear baselines and target ranges?
26. Are experiments/pilots designed with hypotheses, control/comparison, and decision criteria?
27. How frequently are metrics reviewed and translated into process/tooling updates?
28. Is there a repeatable cadence for identifying, prioritizing, and implementing improvements?

---

## Optional Context Questions (for proposal shaping)
29. What is the highest-cost operational bottleneck today (time, quality, margin, or growth constraint)?
30. What must be true in 90 days for this initiative to be considered a clear win?
31. Which stakeholders can block progress, and what evidence do they require to support rollout?
32. What existing projects or constraints could reduce implementation capacity?

---

## Suggested scoring output
- **Question scores:** 1–5 each
- **Domain score:** average of non-N/A questions in domain
- **Overall maturity:** weighted or unweighted domain average
- **Evidence confidence:** % of scored questions with High-confidence evidence
- **Top 3 gaps:** lowest-scoring, highest-impact questions
- **90-day priorities:** mapped to business outcomes and owners
