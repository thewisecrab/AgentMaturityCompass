# AMC Compass Sprint — 5-Day Plan to Close + Collect $5,000

**Sprint window:** Thu Feb 19, 2026 → Mon Feb 23, 2026 (Asia/Calcutta)  
**Primary target:** One signed AMC Compass Sprint at **$5,000 paid in full by Mon Feb 23**  
**Secondary target:** 1–2 qualified opportunities for follow-on offers

## Guardrails (Compliance + Evidence)
- No guaranteed outcome claims (avoid: “guaranteed ROI,” “we will 10x”).
- Use verifiable language: “based on prior engagements,” “we observed,” “typical range.”
- Every outbound claim must map to evidence:
  - Public case proof (link/screenshot/quote)
  - Internal artifact (before/after process docs)
  - Method proof (rubric, scoring logic, deliverable samples)
- Pricing/terms must be explicit in writing before close call.
- Collection target = invoice issued + payment received confirmation.

## Offer Definition (fixed for sprint)
- **Offer:** AMC Compass Sprint (5-day diagnostic + execution roadmap)
- **Price:** $5,000 one-time (paid upfront to start)
- **Core deliverables:**
  1. AI maturity scorecard (current state)
  2. Opportunity map (top 3 high-leverage use cases)
  3. 30/60/90-day roadmap with owner-level actions
  4. Risk + governance checklist (compliance, ops, data)
  5. Executive readout session
- **Best-fit ICP for this sprint:** service businesses/SMB operators with visible delivery bottlenecks and decision-maker access.

## Risk Triggers + Mitigation
- **Low response (<10% by end Day 2):** tighten ICP + increase channel mix + new subject lines.
- **No proposal-stage by end Day 3:** run same-day referral asks from warm network.
- **Procurement delay on Day 4/5:** offer immediate payment link + optional split milestones only if approved.
- **Compliance uncertainty:** remove claim; replace with process/evidence statement.
