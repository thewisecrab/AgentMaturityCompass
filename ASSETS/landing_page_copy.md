## 8) FAQ Section

### How long does the Compass Sprint take?
Most engagements are completed in 2–3 weeks, depending on stakeholder availability and data readiness.

### What do you need from our team?
Typically: a primary point of contact, access to key stakeholders, and relevant planning/operational documents.

### Is this implementation support or strategy only?
The Sprint is strategy-focused and produces a decision-ready roadmap. If implementation support is needed, that can be scoped separately.

### Will this work for smaller or single-site groups?
Yes—if there is clear leadership sponsorship and readiness to act on prioritized recommendations.

### Do you guarantee outcomes?
No. Outcomes depend on many factors, including execution, market conditions, payer dynamics, and organizational constraints.

### Is this legal, financial, or clinical advice?
No. The Compass Sprint provides strategic and operational guidance only. Organizations should consult qualified legal, financial, compliance, and clinical experts as appropriate.

## 9) Compliance-Safe Claims Library

Use these claim patterns across page copy, ads, and sales collateral.

### Preferred (safer) phrasing

- “Designed to help leadership teams make clearer growth decisions.”
- “Built to improve prioritization and execution focus.”
- “Provides a structured 90-day roadmap based on your context.”
- “Supports cross-functional alignment on near-term initiatives.”
- “Aims to reduce strategic drift and decision delays.”

### Avoid / replace

- ❌ “Guaranteed growth” → ✅ “Designed to support growth planning and execution.”
- ❌ “Will increase revenue” → ✅ “Identifies revenue-relevant opportunities and constraints.”
- ❌ “Proven to deliver results in every market” → ✅ “Tailored recommendations based on your market conditions.”
- ❌ “Risk-free” → ✅ “Includes risk and dependency considerations to support informed decisions.”

### Recommended disclaimer block (footer-ready)

The AMC Compass Sprint provides strategic planning support and is not legal, accounting, reimbursement, regulatory, or clinical advice. Any projections or scenario estimates are illustrative and depend on assumptions, execution quality, and external market factors. Past performance does not guarantee future results.
