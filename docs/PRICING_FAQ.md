# PRICING_FAQ.md — Pricing Questions

## Is AMC free?
Yes. The full trust stack is MIT licensed and free: Score, Shield, Enforce, Vault, Watch, Fleet, Passport, Comply, all 14 adapters, 481 CLI commands, browser playground, and CI gates.

## What costs money?
Only **Industry Packs** — 40 sector-specific domain packs for regulated verticals like healthcare, finance, education, and government.

## Do I need Industry Packs?
Not unless you work in a regulated industry and want pre-built, professionally maintained sector-specific diagnostics and compliance mappings. The core trust stack works perfectly without them.

## Can I build my own domain packs?
Yes. AMC's pack system is extensible. You can create custom packs for any domain. The paid Industry Packs are maintained with ongoing regulatory research.

## Will free features become paid later?
No. MIT license is permanent. The trust stack stays free.

## What does Enterprise include?
All 40 Industry Packs, priority support, custom pack development, and deployment assistance.

## Is there a free trial for Industry Packs?
Contact us. We're open to evaluation access for serious buyers.
