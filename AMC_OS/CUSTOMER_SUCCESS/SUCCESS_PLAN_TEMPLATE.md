## SECTION 5 — ESCALATION PATH

> *Use this when the standard path is not moving fast enough. Do not let issues sit >24h without escalation.*

### 5.1 Escalation Triggers

| Trigger | Severity | Escalate To | SLA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sprint gate failure (Pre-Sprint): SOW unsigned, kickoff not confirmed, workspace not provisioned | 🔴 Critical | REV_PROGRAM_MANAGER | Same day |
| Client goes dark: no response for >24h during sprint | 🔴 Critical | REV_CUSTOMER_SUCCESS_MANAGER (CSM escalates internally) | Same day |
| Key stakeholder cancels kickoff or readout | 🟠 High | CSM + REV_PROGRAM_MANAGER | Same day |
| Evidence quality insufficient to score ≥4 dimensions | 🟠 High | REV_QA_LEAD + CSM | Within 4h |
| Scoring dispute (consultant vs. QA lead) | 🟠 High | REV_QA_LEAD (arbiter = AMC_SCORING_GUIDE_v1.md) | Within 4h |
| Client requests scope change during sprint | 🟠 High | REV_PROGRAM_MANAGER + REV_ACCOUNT_EXEC_CLOSER | Same day |
| Legal or compliance question from client | 🟠 High | REV_LEGAL_CONTRACTS | Same day |
| Client expresses significant dissatisfaction (NPS ≤6 or verbal complaint) | 🔴 Critical | CSM + REV_HEAD_OF_SALES | Same day |
| Client disputes final score after readout | 🟡 Medium | CSM → Impl. Specialist review → REV_QA_LEAD | 48h |
| Readout delayed or rescheduled | 🟡 Medium | CSM notifies REV_PROGRAM_MANAGER; reschedule within 72h | Same day |

### 6.2 Signal Categories & Playbook

| Signal Type | Example | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Roadmap Overwhelm** | "We don't have capacity to do this alone" | Propose Option B or C retainer on Day 5 |
| **Speed Urgency** | "We need this done before Q3" | Propose accelerated guided implementation |
| **Score Shock** | Client visibly surprised by low scores; asks "what do we do now?" | Lead with reassurance; immediately pivot to roadmap + retainer |
| **Peer Reference Request** | "Do you work with other companies in our space?" | Provide approved case reference or testimonial |
| **Referral Signal** | "Our CISO at [sister company] has the same problem" | Log name; share referral program after readout |
| **Expansion Domain** | "We have 3 other teams running AI agents not in scope" | Note in expansion log; propose Sprint 2 |
| **Budget Mention** | "We have budget for the rest of the year" | Accelerate retainer conversation; propose multi-sprint package |
| **Champion Building** | Sponsor introduces you to new stakeholder mid-sprint | Engage stakeholder; they may become internal champion |
