## Weekly conversion targets
- **Week 1:** establish baseline CVRs + capture first wave of scorecard leads.

## Content production guardrails
- No guaranteed revenue claims.
- Any performance claim must include context/disclaimer.
- Every post must include one clear CTA only.
- Every link must use UTM tags for experiment attribution.

## Review cadence

- Weekly (Fri): KPI review by asset and channel; reallocate next week’s slots to top performers.
