## Scoring Rubric
**Fit Score (1–10)** composed of:
- **Audience match** (4 pts): % of listeners who are AI practitioners, product leaders, or AI-first founders
- **Topic alignment** (3 pts): depth of coverage on AI agents, AI production readiness, AI governance
- **Booking realism** (2 pts): openness to subject-matter experts (not just celebrity/VC guests)
- **Reach ROI** (1 pt): estimated listener count × conversion relevance
