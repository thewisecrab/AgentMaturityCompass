AI Maturity Company (AMC) today announced the launch of the Compass Sprint, a five-day structured engagement designed to give AI-first teams a scored baseline of where their AI agents stand across four production-critical dimensions — reliability, operator trust design, human oversight architecture, and evidence quality — and a prioritized roadmap for what to address next.

## BODY — CONTEXT / PROBLEM PARAGRAPH

As organizations accelerate the deployment of AI agents into production workflows, many teams encounter a structural gap between an agent that appears to work and one that can be demonstrated to meet production standards for reliability, trustworthiness, and accountability. Without a structured approach to assessing that gap, decisions about deployment readiness are often made informally, without a shared language or scored evidence base. [NOTE: This reflects our observed market position — do not represent as industry statistic or cite as independent research without a verifiable source.]

---

## BODY — PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

The Compass Sprint is a fixed-scope, fixed-price engagement structured to complete in five business days. Deliverables include:

- **Scored maturity baseline:** an assessment across four dimensions — reliability under load, operator trust design, human oversight architecture, and evidence quality
- **Gap analysis:** a ranked list of the highest-impact areas for improvement, specific to the team's deployment context
- **30/60/90-day execution roadmap:** prioritized next steps the team can act on immediately
- **Live stakeholder readout:** a 60-minute session with findings and recommendations for key decision-makers

Pricing is $5,000, fixed. There are no variable scope additions included in the Sprint engagement. Teams can engage the Sprint on a standalone basis with no implementation lock-in.
