## 1. PURPOSE

The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship involving AI agent maturity assessment, advisory services, and related engagements (the "Purpose"). In connection with this Purpose, each Party may disclose certain confidential and proprietary information to the other. This Agreement establishes the terms under which such information will be protected.

---

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

### 2.1 What counts as Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by one Party (the "Disclosing Party") to the other Party (the "Receiving Party") — whether in writing, orally, visually, electronically, or by any other means — that:

(a) is marked or designated as "Confidential," "Proprietary," or a similar label at the time of disclosure; or  
(b) is disclosed orally or in an unmarked form and is identified as confidential at the time of disclosure or within five (5) business days thereafter in a written summary; or  
(c) a reasonable person in the relevant industry would understand to be confidential given the nature of the information and the circumstances of disclosure.

### 2.2 Examples of Confidential Information (non-exhaustive)

- Business strategies, plans, and financial projections
- Product roadmaps, technical architectures, and AI system designs
- Source code, models, training data, evaluation frameworks
- Client lists, prospect lists, and commercial relationships
- Proprietary assessment methodologies, scoring rubrics, and frameworks
- Personnel information and compensation structures
- Pricing, terms, and deal structures

---

## 3. OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECEIVING PARTY

The Receiving Party agrees to:

3.1 **Protect confidentiality.** Hold the Disclosing Party's Confidential Information in strict confidence using at least the same degree of care it uses for its own confidential information of similar sensitivity — and no less than reasonable care.

3.2 **Limit use.** Use Confidential Information solely for evaluating or pursuing the Purpose. Not use it for any competitive purpose or to the disadvantage of the Disclosing Party.

3.3 **Limit disclosure.** Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent from the Disclosing Party. Disclosure within the Receiving Party's organization is permitted only to employees, contractors, or advisors who (a) have a genuine need to know for the Purpose, and (b) are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as those in this Agreement.

3.4 **Notify of breach.** Promptly notify the Disclosing Party in writing upon becoming aware of any unauthorized use or disclosure of Confidential Information. Cooperate reasonably to contain and mitigate the breach.

3.5 **No reverse engineering.** Not reverse-engineer, decompile, or disassemble any software, models, or systems provided as part of Confidential Information.

---

## 4. EXCLUSIONS FROM CONFIDENTIALITY

The obligations in Section 3 do not apply to information that the Receiving Party can demonstrate with contemporaneous documentary evidence:

4.1 Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;

4.2 Was already known to the Receiving Party before disclosure under this Agreement, free of any confidentiality obligation;

4.3 Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of or reference to the Disclosing Party's Confidential Information;

4.4 Is received from a third party who has the right to disclose it without restriction;

4.5 Is required to be disclosed by applicable law, regulation, court order, or government authority — provided that the Receiving Party (a) gives the Disclosing Party advance written notice to the extent legally permitted, (b) cooperates with the Disclosing Party's efforts to seek a protective order or other relief, and (c) discloses only what is legally required.

---

## 5. TERM AND SURVIVAL

5.1 **Term of Agreement.** This Agreement is effective as of the Effective Date and continues for a period of **two (2) years** unless earlier terminated in writing by either Party with thirty (30) days' notice.

5.2 **Survival of obligations.** Confidentiality obligations with respect to Confidential Information disclosed during the Agreement term survive termination for a period of **three (3) years** from the date of initial disclosure of each item of Confidential Information, or until such information falls into the public domain through no fault of the Receiving Party, whichever occurs first.

5.3 **Return or destruction.** Upon request by the Disclosing Party or upon termination, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or certify destruction of all Confidential Information (in any form), except to the extent retention is required by applicable law or regulation.

---

## 6. OWNERSHIP AND NO LICENSE

6.1 All Confidential Information remains the exclusive property of the Disclosing Party. Nothing in this Agreement grants the Receiving Party any license, right, title, or interest in the Confidential Information beyond the limited right to use it for the Purpose.

6.2 No license to any patent, trademark, copyright, or trade secret is granted or implied by this Agreement.

---

## 7. REMEDIES

7.1 **Acknowledgment of harm.** The Parties acknowledge that unauthorized disclosure or use of Confidential Information may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be an inadequate remedy.

7.2 **Equitable relief.** In the event of an actual or threatened breach, the Disclosing Party is entitled to seek injunctive or other equitable relief without posting bond or proving actual damages, in addition to all other legal remedies available.

7.3 **Cumulative rights.** All remedies under this Agreement are cumulative and not exclusive of any other remedies available at law or in equity.

---

## 8. GENERAL PROVISIONS

8.1 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties with respect to confidentiality of information shared for the Purpose and supersedes all prior discussions on this topic.

8.2 **Amendments.** Any modification must be in writing and signed by both Parties.

8.3 **No waiver.** Failure to enforce any provision does not constitute a waiver of future enforcement rights.

8.4 **Severability.** If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions continue in full force.

8.5 **Governing law.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of [GOVERNING JURISDICTION], without regard to conflict-of-law principles.

8.6 **Dispute resolution.** Any dispute arising out of this Agreement shall first be addressed through good-faith negotiation between the Parties. If unresolved within 15 days, the Parties agree to [MEDIATION / ARBITRATION in accordance with [ARBITRATION BODY RULES] / LITIGATION in courts of [JURISDICTION]].

8.8 **No partnership.** Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, agency, employment, or franchise relationship between the Parties.
