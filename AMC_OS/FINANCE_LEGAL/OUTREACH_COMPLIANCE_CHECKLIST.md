# OUTREACH COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

Use this checklist before publishing any outreach email, DM, ad, landing page, webinar invite, proposal, or sales script.

## 1) Claim Safety Gate (Pass/Fail)
- [ ] No guaranteed outcomes (growth, revenue, ROI, conversion, approval, compliance)
- [ ] No absolute words unless provable: always / never / 100% / eliminate risk / foolproof
- [ ] No unverified superlatives: #1 / best / fastest / most accurate
- [ ] No implied regulated advice (investment/legal/tax/accounting/clinical/regulatory)
- [ ] No claim of automatic compliance or replacing legal/compliance review

## 2) Evidence Gate (for any performance claim)
If the asset includes numbers or comparative outcomes:
- [ ] Baseline is stated
- [ ] Time period is stated
- [ ] Population/sample context is stated
- [ ] Data source is documented
- [ ] Claim includes qualifier (e.g., "results vary by context")
- [ ] Reviewer sign-off captured (business + compliance/legal)

## 3) Language Quality Gate
- [ ] Uses qualified phrasing: "designed to," "can help," "supports"
- [ ] States operational/process outcomes (speed, consistency, throughput) vs promises
- [ ] Avoids pressure/manipulative framing in CTAs
- [ ] No misleading omission of assumptions, dependencies, or constraints

## 4) Disclaimer Gate (insert where relevant)
- [ ] "Results are not guaranteed"
- [ ] "Human review and approval required"
- [ ] "Not legal/tax/accounting/regulatory/clinical/investment advice" (as applicable)
- [ ] "Examples are illustrative, not future-performance promises"

## 5) Channel-Specific Quick Checks
### Outreach Emails / DMs
- [ ] Subject line is accurate, not clickbait or deceptive
- [ ] Problem statement tied to observable business context
- [ ] CTA requests conversation, not implied guaranteed outcome

### Sales Calls / Scripts
- [ ] Reps avoid outcome guarantees during objection handling
- [ ] Reps use scenario language (potential upside) with caveats
- [ ] Follow-up summaries preserve qualifiers and disclaimers

### Landing Pages / Ads
- [ ] Hero and CTA avoid deterministic claims
- [ ] Proof sections only include verifiable claims
- [ ] Disclaimer visible near CTA/footer

### Proposals / SOWs
- [ ] Deliverables and acceptance criteria are specific
- [ ] Scope exclusions are explicit
- [ ] Dependencies and client responsibilities are explicit
- [ ] No performance guarantee language in commercial terms

## Fast Red-Flag Rewrite Bank
- ❌ "We will increase your revenue" → ✅ "We will prioritize revenue-relevant opportunities and execution steps"
- ❌ "Guaranteed ROI in 30 days" → ✅ "We target measurable improvements over a defined pilot window; results vary"
- ❌ "Compliant by default" → ✅ "Built to support compliance workflows with mandatory review gates"
- ❌ "Risk-free" → ✅ "Includes explicit risks, assumptions, and dependencies"
