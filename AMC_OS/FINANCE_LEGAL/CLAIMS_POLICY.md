# CLAIMS POLICY (REV_COMPLIANCE_OFFICER)

## Purpose
Set enforceable guardrails for outreach, sales, and marketing claims so all external messaging is truthful, evidence-based, and compliant.

## Scope
Applies to:
- AMC_OS/SALES/*
- AMC_OS/MARKETING/*
- ASSETS/*
- amc_ai_army/* (when used in external-facing messaging)

## Non-Negotiable Rules
1. **No guaranteed outcomes**
   - Prohibited: "guaranteed results," "will increase revenue," "risk-free," "100% success."
2. **No implied legal/regulatory certification**
   - Prohibited: "compliant by default," "automatic compliance," "SEBI-compliant guaranteed" (or equivalent).
3. **No investment/advisory implication**
   - Must not imply investment, legal, tax, accounting, reimbursement, clinical, or regulatory advice unless explicitly authorized and licensed.
4. **No unverifiable superlatives**
   - Prohibited without proof: "#1," "best," "most accurate," "fastest."
5. **No absolute language unless provable and documented**
   - Avoid: always, never, eliminate, foolproof, zero risk.

## Allowed Claim Types (Preferred)
- Process improvement and workflow support claims
- Throughput, consistency, and turnaround framing
- Decision support and prioritization framing
- Contextual, qualified statements ("designed to," "can help," "supports")

## Conditional Numeric Claims (Evidence Required)
Numeric claims (%, time saved, conversion uplift, ROI) are allowed only if all are present:
- Baseline definition
- Time window
- Sample size/context
- Source of truth (case/pilot/report)
- Reviewer approval (business owner + legal/compliance)
- Variability qualifier ("results vary by context")

## Mandatory Disclaimers (Use as Applicable)
1. **No guarantees**
   - "Outcomes depend on implementation quality, data inputs, and market/contextual factors; results are not guaranteed."
2. **Human oversight**
   - "All outputs require client review and approval, including legal/compliance review where applicable."
3. **Not regulated advice**
   - "This material is for strategic/operational information and is not legal, tax, accounting, regulatory, reimbursement, clinical, or investment advice."
4. **Illustrative examples only**
   - "Examples are illustrative and should not be interpreted as promises of future performance."

## Claim Risk Tiers
- **Green (safe):** Process/support language with qualifiers
- **Amber (review required):** Comparative or performance framing without hard numbers
- **Red (block):** Guarantees, compliance certification claims, investment-return claims, absolute statements

## Required Pre-Publish Workflow
1. Draft owner completes checklist (see OUTREACH_COMPLIANCE_CHECKLIST.md)
2. Claim-evidence links added for every numeric/performance claim
3. Compliance reviewer signs off for Amber/Red-adjacent language
4. Final asset includes relevant disclaimer block
5. Archive version + approver in campaign/proposal notes

## Standard Rewrites (Use These)
- ❌ "We guarantee growth" → ✅ "Designed to improve growth planning and execution focus"
- ❌ "Will increase revenue" → ✅ "Identifies revenue-relevant opportunities and constraints"
- ❌ "Fully compliant by default" → ✅ "Supports compliance workflows with structured review gates"
- ❌ "Risk-free" → ✅ "Includes risk/dependency considerations to support informed decisions"
- ❌ "Best/fastest solution" → ✅ "Built for faster decision-ready outputs in defined workflows"
