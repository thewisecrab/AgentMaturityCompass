## 1. OVERVIEW

The Compass Sprint is a fixed-scope, fixed-price engagement priced at **$5,000 USD** (or equivalent in agreed local currency at the time of booking).

All payment terms are explicit, non-negotiable in their structure (options available), and communicated in writing before engagement kickoff. No work begins without a signed Statement of Work (SOW) and confirmed initial payment.

---

## 2. PAYMENT OPTIONS

Three structures are available. The default recommendation is **Option A (Full Upfront)** — it unlocks the earliest kickoff, the lowest administrative overhead, and the clearest risk profile for both parties.

---

### OPTION A — Full Upfront (Recommended)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Total** | $5,000 |
| **Payment 1** | $5,000 — due upon SOW signature, before kickoff |
| **Payment 2** | N/A |
| **Kickoff eligibility** | Within 48 hours of payment confirmed |
| **Discount** | None — this is the standard price |
| **Best for** | Clients with streamlined AP processes; clients who want earliest kickoff |

**Why we recommend this:**  
Payment in full removes scheduling risk, eliminates mid-sprint invoice delays, and allows AMC to fully resource the engagement without payment contingency. It is the cleanest path to a 5-business-day delivery.

---

### OPTION B — 50/50 Split

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Total** | $5,000 |
| **Payment 1** | $2,500 — due upon SOW signature, before kickoff |
| **Payment 2** | $2,500 — due upon delivery of final Compass Sprint report and readout |
| **Kickoff eligibility** | Within 48 hours of Payment 1 confirmed |
| **Delivery trigger** | Final report + 60-minute readout session scheduled |
| **Best for** | Clients with two-step approval cycles; clients new to AMC |

**Terms:**  
- Delivery of the final Compass Sprint report is contingent on Payment 2 being received within 3 business days of delivery invoice.
- If Payment 2 is not received within 7 calendar days of report delivery, the report and associated IP are withheld until payment is cleared.
- IP transfer (Section 6) is conditional on full payment.

---

### OPTION C — Milestone-Based (Three Tranches)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Total** | $5,000 |
| **Payment 1** | $2,000 — due upon SOW signature, before kickoff |
| **Payment 2** | $1,500 — due upon delivery of mid-sprint progress update (Day 3) |
| **Payment 3** | $1,500 — due upon delivery of final report and readout (Day 5) |
| **Kickoff eligibility** | Within 48 hours of Payment 1 confirmed |
| **Best for** | Clients with formal milestone-based procurement; enterprise clients |

**Milestone definitions:**

| Milestone | Description | Trigger for next payment |
|---|---|---|
| M1 — Kickoff | Intake call completed; scope confirmed; data access established | Clears Payment 1 (paid in advance) |
| M2 — Mid-Sprint Update | Day 3 interim findings memo delivered (draft scoring + top gaps identified) | Payment 2 due within 2 business days |
| M3 — Final Delivery | Full Compass Sprint report + 60-min readout session delivered | Payment 3 due within 3 business days |

**Terms:**  
- If Payment 2 is not received within 5 business days of M2 delivery, Day 4–5 work pauses until payment clears.
- If Payment 3 is not received within 7 calendar days of M3 delivery, final deliverables are withheld.
- IP transfer (Section 6) is conditional on full payment.

## 5. LATE PAYMENT POLICY

5.1 **Grace period:** 3 calendar days past invoice due date.

5.2 **Late fee:** After the grace period, a late payment fee of **1.5% per month** (or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, whichever is lower) accrues on the outstanding balance, calculated from the original due date.

5.3 **Work suspension:** For Option B and Option C, AMC reserves the right to suspend delivery work if a milestone payment is overdue by more than 5 business days. AMC will provide written notice before suspension.

5.4 **Final deliverable hold:** The final Compass Sprint report and associated intellectual property will not be transferred until all outstanding amounts are paid in full.

5.5 **Collections:** If payment is not received within 30 calendar days of the due date, AMC may refer the account to a collections process and/or pursue available legal remedies. Client is responsible for reasonable collections costs incurred by AMC.

5.6 **No penalty for early payment:** Clients paying ahead of schedule receive no discount but experience faster kickoff and delivery scheduling.

## 6. IP TRANSFER — CONDITIONAL ON FULL PAYMENT

6.1 AMC retains all intellectual property rights in all deliverables, methodologies, scoring frameworks, and work product until full payment is received.

6.2 **Upon receipt of full payment**, AMC grants Client a **perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license** to use the Compass Sprint deliverables (scorecard, gap analysis, execution roadmap, and related reports) for the Client's internal business purposes.

6.3 **What AMC retains regardless of payment:** AMC retains rights to its underlying assessment methodology, scoring rubric, maturity framework, and any general intellectual capital developed or refined during the engagement. The Client's license is to the specific deliverables, not to AMC's proprietary methodology.

6.4 **White-label exception:** Any use of AMC deliverables in client-facing or commercial contexts (e.g., agencies white-labeling the report to their own clients) requires a separate written license agreement with AMC.

6.5 This IP provision survives termination of any engagement agreement.

## 7. REFUND POLICY

7.1 **Before kickoff (before Day 1 intake call):**  
If Client cancels in writing before the kickoff call, AMC will refund the full initial payment received, less a $250 administrative processing fee.

7.2 **After kickoff, before Day 3 mid-sprint update:**  
If Client cancels after kickoff but before mid-sprint update delivery, AMC will refund 50% of amounts paid, less costs incurred to date. AMC will provide an itemized statement of costs within 5 business days.

7.3 **After Day 3 mid-sprint update delivery:**  
No refund is available. AMC will have delivered substantive work product at this stage. Client receives all work completed to date.

7.4 **AMC-initiated cancellation:**  
If AMC cancels the engagement for reasons within AMC's control (excluding client non-cooperation or failure to provide required access), AMC will refund 100% of amounts paid within 5 business days.

7.5 **Force majeure:**  
In the event of force majeure preventing completion, the Parties will negotiate in good faith. AMC will refund amounts paid less reasonable costs incurred; no penalty applies to either party.

7.6 **Refund processing time:** 5–10 business days to original payment method.

## 8. PREREQUISITES FOR KICKOFF (DELIVERY-READINESS GATE)

No Compass Sprint kickoff is scheduled until ALL of the following are confirmed:

- [ ] Signed Statement of Work (SOW) received
- [ ] Initial payment confirmed (cleared funds, not just initiated)
- [ ] Intake questionnaire submitted by Client
- [ ] Point of contact (technical + commercial) confirmed by Client
- [ ] Required data access or system access credentials agreed in writing

**Kickoff SLA:** AMC commits to scheduling kickoff within **48 hours** of all prerequisites being met.

## INTERNAL USAGE NOTES

### Which option to recommend in proposal
- **Default:** Option A. Present it first; frame it as the fastest path to kickoff.
- **Fallback:** Option B. Use when client signals budget approval complexity but has confirmed budget.
- **Enterprise / Agency:** Option C. Use when formal procurement requires milestone-based release.

### How to present payment terms on a sales call
> "We have three payment structures — full upfront (which gets you kicked off fastest), a 50/50 split with payment at delivery, or milestone-based if your procurement requires it. Most clients go with full upfront because it's simpler and we start within 48 hours. Which of those works best for your team?"

### Red flags to watch for
- Client asking for 30+ day payment terms on a 5-day sprint (raise to founder for approval or decline)
- Client hesitating on SOW signature but requesting work to begin (do not start)
- Client requesting deliverables before payment 2 clears (do not send final report)
