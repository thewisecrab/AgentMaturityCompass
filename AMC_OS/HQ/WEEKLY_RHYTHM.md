| Day | Priority | What gets reviewed | Owner | Escalation trigger |
|---|---|---|---|---|

## Daily execution checkpoints (non-negotiable)
- **Morning (standup prep and review):** refresh SCOREBOARD and assign blockers.
- **Midday pipeline check:** compare to daily pacing; reallocate capacity when below trigger.
- **EOD close-out:** scoreboard update + one decision + one bottleneck + next action owner.
- **Compliance/QA gate:** no outbound/proposal asset released with unresolved red flags.

## Escalation owners
- **COO Orchestrator** owns operational correction plan.
- **Sales lead** owns volume and reply conversion misses.
- **RevOps lead** owns CRM/data quality misses.
- **Compliance/QA owners** own legal/trust risks.
- **Finance lead** owns cash/risk-to-collection updates and invoice-readiness issues.
