# NORTH STAR

AMC helps teams ship AI agents safely and profitably with evidence-backed maturity scoring and execution roadmaps.

## 90-day outcomes
1. Close initial paid Compass Sprint engagements.
2. Convert Sprint outcomes into recurring maturity retainers.
3. Stand up repeatable delivery and benchmarkable evidence workflows.

## Success signals
- Cash collected
- Qualified pipeline velocity
- Delivery cycle time
- Maturity delta and evidence quality


## $5,000 Collection Alignment Rule
Until $5,000 is collected, the org runs in **CASH FIRST** mode.

Allowed work must directly increase probability of $5k collection within 5 days by improving:
- Pipeline volume/quality (meetings booked)
- Conversion (proposal-to-close)
- Delivery readiness (reduce buyer risk)

If a task does not support these, defer it.

Daily scoreboard must track:
- # new qualified leads added
- # outreaches sent (personalized, compliant)
- # replies
- # calls booked
- # calls completed
- # proposals sent
- $ in weighted pipeline
- $ collected
