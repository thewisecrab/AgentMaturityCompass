# OFFERS — IMMEDIATE CLOSE MENU

## Offer Architecture
Use a 3-path structure to reduce indecision and accelerate commitment.

1. **Sprint** (fast diagnostic + plan)
2. **Pilot** (targeted execution with measurable win)
3. **Retainer** (ongoing operating cadence)

---

## 1) AMC Compass Sprint (5 business days)
**Best for:** Teams needing executive clarity and a launch-ready plan.

- **Price:** $5,000 fixed
- **Timeline:** 5 business days
- **Deliverables:**
  - AI/agent maturity scorecard
  - Evidence-backed gap analysis
  - Prioritized 30/60/90 execution roadmap
  - Executive readout + decision memo
- **Client inputs required:**
  - Stakeholder interviews (2–4)
  - Current workflow + tooling access
  - Baseline KPI snapshot
- **Exclusions:** Build/implementation work not included
- **Close CTA:** "Approve today, kickoff in 48 hours."

## 2) AMC Rapid Pilot (14 days)
**Best for:** Teams with one urgent use-case and clear owner.

- **Price:** $12,000 fixed
- **Timeline:** 14 calendar days
- **Deliverables:**
  - One production-priority workflow scoped and shipped
  - Quality/trust controls + escalation path
  - KPI baseline vs. post-pilot readout
  - Operator playbook + handoff session
- **Success criteria (example):**
  - Cycle-time reduction, defect reduction, or conversion lift (defined in SOW)
- **Client inputs required:**
  - Decision-maker approval
  - Single accountable implementation owner
  - Access to required systems/data
- **Exclusions:** Multi-workflow rollout, custom platform engineering
- **Close CTA:** "Reserve start slot this week; limited concurrent pilots."

## 3) AMC Continuous Maturity (Monthly)
**Best for:** Teams scaling multiple workflows with governance.

- **Price:** $3,000–$15,000/month (tiered by scope)
- **Term:** 3-month minimum recommended
- **Deliverables:**
  - Monthly maturity rescoring
  - Launch gating + QA/compliance checkpoints
  - KPI drift monitoring and corrective plans
  - Quarterly strategy + roadmap refresh
- **Cadence:** Weekly ops sync + monthly executive review
- **Exclusions:** New net-new engineering outside agreed scope
- **Close CTA:** "Select scope tier now; kickoff date locked on signature."

---

## Packaging Rules for Higher Close Rate
- Always present **Good / Better / Best** in one view.
- Anchor on business cost of delay before pricing.
- Tie every line item to an explicit outcome metric.
- Include start date and first milestone in proposal body.
- Keep terms simple: scope, timeline, fee, acceptance criteria, exclusions.

## Commercial Guardrails
- No guaranteed revenue/income promises.
- No vague deliverables; must be checkable.
- If buyer lacks authority/timeline, sell Sprint first or disqualify.

## Fast Concession Ladder (protect margin)
1. Adjust payment schedule (not total value)
2. Narrow scope to highest-ROI workflow
3. Shift to Pilot before Retainer
4. Add decision deadline for any concession

## Proposal Minimum Contents (same-day issue)
- Problem statement + quantified impact
- Scope and deliverables
- Timeline + milestones
- Price + payment terms
- Acceptance criteria
- Exclusions
- Decision deadline + kickoff date
