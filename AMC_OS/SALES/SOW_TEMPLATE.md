## 4) Out of Scope (Hard Boundaries)
The following are excluded unless changed via Change Order:
- Channels/products/regions not listed in Section 3
- New integration builds and custom app development beyond listed items
- Unlimited revisions or redesign cycles beyond agreed cap
- Data remediation/cleanup of legacy systems unless explicitly included
- Legal opinions, tax advice, or regulator liaison work

## 5) Client Responsibilities (Conditions for Success)
Client shall:
1. Provide required system access, SMEs, and timely approvals.
2. Assign one decision-maker authorized to approve milestones.
3. Deliver dependencies listed in Exhibit A by agreed dates.
4. Ensure lawful rights to all provided content/data.

**Dependency SLA:** Client delays exceeding ___ business days auto-shift timeline and may trigger re-planning fees.

## 6) Project Plan, Governance & Communication
- **Cadence:** Weekly status meeting + async tracker updates.
- **Reporting:** Risks, blockers, milestone status, next actions.
- **Escalation path:** Project Lead → Account Owner → Executive Sponsor.
- **Decision log location:** `AMC_OS/` agreed folder or client PM tool.

## 7) Acceptance Testing & Sign-off
### 7.1 UAT Method
- AMC submits deliverable with acceptance checklist.
- Client tests against criteria in Section 7.2 within ___ business days.

### 7.2 Acceptance Criteria
- Deliverable materially conforms to documented requirements.
- Critical defects: 0 open
- Major defects: ≤ ___ with workaround
- Minor defects: logged for scheduled fix

### 7.3 Deemed Acceptance
If client does not reject in writing with reproducible evidence within the review window, deliverable is deemed accepted.

## 9) Change Order Procedure
Any change to scope, assumptions, timeline, resources, SLA, or deliverables requires:
1) written CR request,
2) impact analysis (cost/time/risk),
3) signed approval before implementation.

## 10) Risk Allocation & Liability
- AMC is not responsible for performance impacts from third-party platform/policy changes.
- AMC is not responsible for outcomes dependent on client-provided incomplete/incorrect inputs.
- Each party warrants authority to enter this SOW.
- AMC aggregate liability under this SOW is capped at fees paid under this SOW in prior ___ months.
- No indirect, special, incidental, or consequential damages.
