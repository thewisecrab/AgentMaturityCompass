## 4) Explicit Out-of-Scope (Boundary Guardrails)
The following are **not included** unless added via signed Change Order:
- New integrations not listed in Section 3
- Net-new strategy outside agreed channel(s)
- Creative/engineering revisions beyond revision caps
- Procurement of third-party tools/licenses
- Legal/compliance review outside standard checks
- On-site support, weekend support, or SLA upgrades (unless priced separately)

## 5) Assumptions & Client Dependencies
- Client provides required access/approvals within ___ business days.
- Client supplies brand assets, legal disclaimers, and historical data as available.
- Delays in dependencies may shift timeline and re-baseline milestone dates.
- Any missing assumptions should be listed in `AMC_OS/HQ/ASSUMPTIONS.md` before signature.

## 7) Acceptance Criteria (Signature-Ready)
A deliverable is accepted when **all** conditions are met:
1. Delivered artifact matches scope description in Section 3.
2. Quality checklist completed and shared.
3. Client UAT feedback submitted within ___ business days.
4. Any defects are classified as Critical/Major/Minor and resolved per agreed SLA.
5. If no written rejection is received within ___ business days of delivery, item is deemed accepted.

## 8) Commercials

  - ___% at milestone M2
  - ___% on final acceptance
- **Taxes:** Extra as applicable
- **Late payment:** ___% per month (or max lawful amount)

## 9) Risk, Compliance & Liability Clauses
- AMC is not liable for outcomes driven by platform algorithm/policy changes.
- AMC is not liable for delays caused by client-side dependency slippage.
- AMC liability is capped at fees paid in prior ___ months under this proposal/SOW.
- Neither party is liable for indirect/consequential damages.
- Force majeure events re-baseline delivery commitments.
- Confidentiality and data handling obligations survive termination for ___ months.

## 10) Change Control
Any request that changes scope, timeline, effort, assumptions, or deliverables requires:
1) written Change Request,
2) impact note (cost/timeline/risk),
3) mutual written approval before execution.

## 11) Term, Termination, and Pause Rules
- Initial term: ___
- Termination for convenience: ___ days written notice.
- Early termination: client pays for work completed + committed non-cancellable costs.
- Project pause > ___ days may require re-onboarding fee and timeline reset.
