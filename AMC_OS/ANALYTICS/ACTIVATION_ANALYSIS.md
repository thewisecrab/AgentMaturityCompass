## 1. DEFINING "ACTIVATED" FOR AMC

### Core Activation Question
*What is the minimum set of behaviors that proves a client received genuine value from the Sprint — and is positioned to expand?*

### Activation Definition (Two Levels)

#### Level 1 — Basic Activation (Sprint Delivery Complete)
A client is **Level 1 Activated** when all of the following occur:
1. ✅ **Readout call completed** — Day 5 readout delivered with ≥2 client stakeholders attending
2. ✅ **Maturity score received and acknowledged** — client explicitly confirms they received and understand their score
3. ✅ **Top 3 risks identified and named** — client can articulate at least 1 top risk in their own words
4. ✅ **90-day roadmap handed off** — client has the roadmap and at least one action has an assigned owner internally

**Level 1 is the minimum viable activation. It proves AMC delivered what was promised.**

#### Level 2 — Deep Activation (Value Realized)
A client is **Level 2 Activated** when, within 30 days of sprint completion:
1. ✅ **At least 1 roadmap action initiated** — client has started (or completed) at least one recommended action from the 90-day plan
2. ✅ **Internal advocacy created** — sponsor shares sprint output with ≥1 executive peer or sends a reference
3. ✅ **Scoring benchmark understood** — client uses AMC maturity language internally ("we're at L2 on observability")
4. ✅ **Retainer conversation held** — client has engaged in at least one conversation about ongoing maturity support (regardless of outcome)

**Level 2 Activated clients are expansion-ready. They've internalized the framework and see ongoing value.**

## 3. SIGNALS THAT PREDICT ACTIVATION vs. CHURN

### Positive Activation Signals (Leading Indicators)

| Signal | When Observed | Weight | Action |
|--------|--------------|--------|--------|
| Client submits evidence >80% complete before Day 0 | Pre-sprint | High | Fast-track; high engagement correlates with value realization |
| CTO/VP attends Day 5 readout (not just project manager) | Day 5 | Very High | Executive buy-in = retainer likelihood ↑ |
| Client asks follow-up questions within 24h of readout | Day 5–6 | High | Strike while hot; book retainer conversation same week |
| Client mentions the sprint to a peer without prompting | Day 7–30 | Very High | Referral and expansion both highly likely |
| Client starts a roadmap action and reports back | Day 14–30 | High | Deep activation; proof they trust the roadmap |
| Client uses AMC maturity vocabulary internally | Day 7–30 | Medium | Framework adoption = stickiness |
| NPS or satisfaction score ≥8/10 on post-sprint survey | Day 5 | High | Reliable expansion predictor |

### Churn Risk Signals (Early Warning)

| Signal | When Observed | Risk Level | Intervention |
|--------|--------------|------------|-------------|
| Evidence intake <50% at Day 0 gate | Pre-sprint | High | Do not start sprint; escalate to CSM + contact client sponsor |
| Key decision-maker absent from Day 5 readout | Day 5 | High | Record call; send personal re-engagement video within 24h |
| No response to post-readout email within 48h | Day 5–7 | Medium | CSM phone call; re-send summary with explicit next step |
| Client says "roadmap looks great" but assigns no internal owner | Day 5–7 | Medium-High | Book a specific "roadmap owner assignment" call within 5 days |
| Client expresses score surprise negatively ("this seems too low") | Day 5 | Medium | Schedule evidence review call; walk through scoring rationale |
| No engagement on Day 30 check-in | Day 30 | High | Escalate to account manager for in-person/video re-engagement |
| Client cancels check-in twice | Day 30–45 | Very High | Executive re-engagement; consider offering 30-min "quick wins" session free |

## 4. INTERVENTIONS FOR AT-RISK CLIENTS

### Intervention Playbook by Risk Signal

#### RISK: Low evidence submission (pre-sprint or Day 1)
**Intervention:** CSM personal phone call (not email) to client sponsor within 2 hours of missed deadline.  
**Script:** "We want to make sure your Sprint delivers maximum value. The evidence items we need are [top 3 items]. Can we spend 15 minutes now to find the right person internally to pull these?"  
**Escalation:** If still incomplete at Day 1 gate → delay sprint start; document in BLOCKERS.md

#### RISK: Decision-maker absent from readout
**Intervention:** Record readout; send personalized 3-minute video summary from lead consultant directly to the absent executive (not via project manager).  
**Script:** "Hi [Name], I wanted to give you a personal 3-minute summary of what we found during your team's sprint this week. [Key finding + risk + opportunity]. We'd love 20 minutes with you to walk through the roadmap before your team moves forward."  
**Follow-up:** If no response in 5 days → escalate to account manager for in-person outreach

#### RISK: No roadmap owner assigned
**Intervention:** Do not let the readout call end without naming an owner for at least 1 quick win.  
**In-call prompt:** "Before we wrap up, I'd like to make sure at least one of these actions has a named owner. Quick wins #1 and #2 are designed to take less than 2 weeks — who on your team would own one of these?"  
**Post-call follow-up:** Include named owners in post-readout email; confirm in 7-day check-in

#### RISK: Client disputes a score
**Intervention:** Do not change score on the call. Say: "Let's capture that — I'll review the evidence we used for this dimension with you async and share my notes."  
**Within 48h:** Schedule 30-min evidence review call; bring scoring worksheet + evidence citations  
**Resolution:** If new evidence is provided, rescore with documentation; if not, keep original score and explain methodology

#### RISK: No engagement at 30-day check-in
**Intervention (Day 30):** CSM sends personalized "quick win check-in" email:  
"Hi [Name], 30 days ago we wrapped up your Compass Sprint. I wanted to check in on [Quick Win #1] — has your team had a chance to kick this off? We also have some updated thinking on [specific finding] that might be useful."  
**Offer:** Free 20-min async "how's the roadmap going?" video message — low commitment, high goodwill  
**Escalation (Day 45 no response):** Account executive + executive sponsor re-engagement; consider offering a mini re-score session (30 min, free, for retention)

| Level 2 Activation rate (Day 30) | ≥60% | CRM: ≥1 action initiated + retainer conversation held |

| Day 30 check-in completion | ≥80% | CSM meeting log |
| Retainer conversion (from activated clients) | ≥40% | CRM: Sprint→Retainer deals |
