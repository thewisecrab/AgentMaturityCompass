# CLAIM REGISTRY — AMC VERIFIED CLAIMS
**Owner:** REV_COMPLIANCE_OFFICER  
**Version:** 1.0 — 2026-02-18  
**Purpose:** Single source of truth for every claim AMC makes in outbound, marketing, proposals, and sales conversations. Every claim has a confidence tier and source. No claim may be used in external-facing material without an entry here.

---

## CONFIDENCE TIERS

| Tier | Label | Definition | Usage Rules |
|---|---|---|---|
| **HIGH** | Verified | Definitionally true, contractually scoped, or structurally logical — does not require external data | Use freely in all channels with normal claim language |
| **MEDIUM** | Directional/Estimated | Consistent with established practice, expert consensus, or reasonable inference — not proven in AMC pilots yet | Use with qualified language: "typically," "tends to," "in most cases," "teams often find" |
| **LOW** | Hypothesis Only | Working assumption not yet validated by AMC or external evidence | Do NOT use in external messaging. Internal use only. Label as "hypothesis" in any internal doc. |

---

## MANDATORY USAGE RULE
Any MEDIUM or LOW claim used in outbound MUST be softened with a qualifier. Any claim not in this registry must be submitted for review before use.
