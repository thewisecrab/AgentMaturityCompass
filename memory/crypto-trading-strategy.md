## STRATEGY 1: Leveraged Futures Scalping (Primary)

### Setup
- **Platform:** Binance Futures (USDT-M Perpetual)
- **Pairs:** BTC/USDT, ETH/USDT (highest liquidity, tightest spreads)
- **Leverage:** 10x-20x (NOT higher — liquidation too close)
- **Timeframe:** 5m-15m charts for entries, 1h-4h for trend direction

### Entry Rules
1. **Trend-following only** — Never counter-trend with leverage
2. **Wait for pullbacks** — Enter on retracements to key levels (EMA20, EMA50, support/resistance)
3. **Volume confirmation** — Only enter when volume is above 20-period average
4. **Momentum indicators:** RSI divergence, MACD crossover on 15m chart

### Risk Management (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
- **Max risk per trade:** 10% of account (with $50, that's $5)
- **Stop loss:** ALWAYS set. 1-2% move against you at 20x = 20-40% of position
- **Take profit targets:** Scale out — 50% at 2:1 R:R, 25% at 3:1, let 25% run
- **Daily loss limit:** 20% of account. Hit it? Done for the day.
- **Move stop to breakeven** after 1:1 R:R reached

### Position Sizing at Different Account Levels
| Account Size | Max Position (20x) | Stop Loss Distance | Risk per Trade |
|---|---|---|---|
| $50 | $1,000 notional | 0.5% | $5 |
| $100 | $2,000 notional | 0.5% | $10 |
| $500 | $10,000 notional | 0.5% | $50 |
| $2,000 | $20,000 notional | 0.5% | $100 (reduced to 5%) |

### Ideal Setups
1. **Breakout retests:** Price breaks key level, retests it as support/resistance, enter with tight stop
2. **EMA bounce:** In uptrend, price pulls back to EMA20/50 on 15m, bounces with volume
3. **Funding rate plays:** When funding rate is extremely negative (shorts paying longs), go long with trend
4. **Liquidation cascades:** After major liquidation event, enter in direction of the cascade for continuation

### Account Configuration
1. Create Binance account (KYC required for futures in most regions)
2. Deposit $50 USDT (via P2P if bank transfer not available in India)
3. Transfer to Futures wallet
4. Set leverage to 10x initially (not higher until profitable)
5. Enable "Position Mode: Hedge" (allows simultaneous long/short)

### Key Binance Features to Use
- **Grid trading bot** — For ranging markets, set and forget
- **TWAP orders** — For larger positions without slippage
- **Trailing stop** — To ride trends automatically
- **Cross vs Isolated margin** — Use ISOLATED always (limits loss to position, not entire account)

### Fees
- Spot: 0.1% maker/taker (0.075% with BNB discount)
- Futures: 0.02% maker / 0.04% taker
- Always use limit orders to pay maker fees

## WHAT COULD GO WRONG (AND WILL)

1. **Liquidation** — At 20x, a 5% move against you = gone. ISOLATED margin + stop loss = mandatory.
2. **Overtrading** — Taking 20 trades/day destroys profits through fees and bad decisions
3. **Revenge trading** — After a loss, the urge to "make it back" leads to bigger losses
4. **Black swan** — Exchange hack, regulatory crackdown, flash crash. Can't prevent, can limit exposure.
5. **Scam coins** — Anything promising guaranteed returns is a scam. Period.
